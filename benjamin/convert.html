Oscar Health
Below is a small selection of projects I've worked on at Oscar Health. I've contributed to brand design, product design, and research.
ID card concepts
Sole designer · Summer 2017

I comped some ID cards to share with our agency partner, Something New. Here are just a few of the versions I played around with. I passed these designs off to Something New, and they started using these explorations to plan for the new and improved welcome kit.

In-app partnership animations
Sole designer · Summer 2017

I created animations to convey in-app logo lockup opportunities for our Cleveland Clinic and Humana partnerships.

Partnership brand books
Sole designer · Summer 2017

I built guideline decks to help teams across our organizations use our co-branding in a variety of contexts without requiring approvals for logo and brand use in every context. Our intention was to use these brand books as templates for future co-branded partnerships, integrations, and joint ventures.

Concierge training certificates
Sole designer · Summer 2017

I was tasked with designing a certificate to present to individuals who have completed 4-week Concierge service training in Tempe, Arizona. I made about 50 versions across different visual styles and arrived at the final layout with the help of several crits.

Provider profile
Sole designer · Spring 2019

Restructured the provider profile on web to handle extensive experimentation and growth. The new design has since scaled easily to accommodate additional information, such as data around cultural competency, office hours, and our recently launched virtual primary care offering, without compromising legibility.

Drug profile
Sole designer · Fall 2018

Redesigned the drug profile on mobile to clearly communicate the exact amount a member would have to pay for a specific drug.

Search filters
Collaborator (1 of 2) · Fall 2019

Search filters were originally hidden behind a click on the search results page. We decided to expose them all in a row above search results so that they're visible while browsing and easy to adjust.

Vitals logging
Sole designer · Spring 2021

As part of the virtual primary care pilot, members received kits containing a blood pressure monitor. We launched our vitals logging feature, allowing members to digitally record, track, and share their blood pressure and heart rate with their virtual PCP.
